What Is NLP?

Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP) is a powerful approach to communication, personal development, and psychotherapy that explores the relationships between how we think (neuro), how we communicate (linguistic), and our behavioral patterns (programming). Developed from the study of exceptional communicators and therapists, NLP provides practical tools for creating positive change in your life.
